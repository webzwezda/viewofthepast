Nikolai Gogol

Nikolai Vasilievich Gogol (31 March 1809 – 4 March 1852) was a Russian dramatist of Ukrainian origin.

Gogol was born in the Ukrainian Cossack town of Sorochyntsi,[4] in Poltava Governorate of the Russian Empire. Wiki Gogol
